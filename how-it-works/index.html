Learn what's happening inside the AI that makes navigating data so much easier.

Neural Data Fabric

Using state-of-the-art deep learning techniques, Resolute relates and enhances unconnected data sets by automatically capturing similarity and ranking, allowing you get critical insights from your data. Resolute reads and understands the context of each document, learning what terms are the most important and which arenâ€™t, making meaningful connections for your data easier than ever.

Resolute enables users to quickly search and perform analytics across multiple domains and various data types by assigning a neural fingerprint to each document, which allows for downstream benefits such as dataset mapping and semantic search capabilities helping our users find insights and make the most sense out of their data.

Data Sources

Patents

Total coverage from over 104 Patent offices, with over 59 million patent families from as early as the 1900s. Full text data from multiple sources including the USPTO, WIPO, EP, AT, CA, CH GB and ES.

Companies

Auto-generated snapshots of more than 2.5 million companies, using venture capital data and mined patent and financial document information.

Scientific Publications

Full text and abstracts for more than 28 million scientific publications including multiple areas of research such as biomedical, engineering, chemicals and more from over 8000 journals.

Tech Transfer

More than 50,000 early and late stage technologies available for licensing from over 1000 research institutions and universities from around the world.

Grants

Government awarded grants including abstract, contract details, and business information for more than 1 million grants.

Clinical Trials

Complete data on conditions, interventions, outcomes and more for over 370,000 clinical trials, completed and ongoing.

Custom Data

Any document or data source, structured or unstructured, can be ingested by Resolute. Some examples include customer feedback, lab data, technology profiles, competitive research reports. Integration also included for 3rd party data providers such as Dropbox, Google Drive and Sharepoint.

Cognitive Search

Resolute offers a comprehensive suite of analytics, visualizations, and AI-guided mining tools out-of-the-box. With our deep learning algorithms ingesting your data, searches become more personalized allowing for faster retrieval and analysis. Additionally, automatic tags and categories make navigating your data easier than ever.
